# LeCoin Physical Coin Design Specifications

## Overview

Custom commemorative challenge coin for LeCoin Founding Supporters. Each coin is uniquely numbered (1-100) and represents a friend or family member who believed in LeDesign before it launched.

---

## Coin Specifications

**Dimensions**:
- **Diameter**: 1.75 inches (44mm) - Standard challenge coin size
- **Thickness**: 3mm (standard)
- **Weight**: ~28-32 grams (feels substantial)

**Material Options**:

1. **Bronze Antique Finish** (Recommended - $8-10/coin)
   - Classic, timeless look
   - Warm tone matches the "friends helping friends" vibe
   - Ages beautifully

2. **Dual Plating** (Premium - $12-15/coin)
   - Gold-plated raised areas
   - Antique bronze recessed areas
   - Creates depth and contrast

3. **Silver-plated** (Budget - $5-7/coin)
   - Clean, modern look
   - Matches tech/engineering theme

**Recommended Finish**: Bronze Antique with optional gold highlights on the "L" logo

- Multiple enamel colors
- Blue, gold, white accents

- **Cost**: ~$12-15/coin for 25 pieces

**Recommendation**: Option 2 (Selective Enamel) - Best balance of beauty and cost

## Ordering Process

### Step 1: Choose Manufacturer

**Recommended**: CustomChallengeCoins.net
- Good quality/price ratio
- 2-3 week turnaround
- Free design proofs
- Easy online ordering

**Alternative**: Coins4Anything.com
- Higher quality
- More expensive
- 3-4 week turnaround

### Step 2: Submit Design

They'll need:
1. **Vector artwork** (I'll create SVG files for you)
2. **Text specifications** (fonts, sizes)
3. **Color codes** (PMS colors for enamel)
4. **Finish preference** (antique bronze)
5. **Quantity**: 25-30 coins (order extras for replacements)

### Step 3: Approve Proofs

- They send digital proof for approval
- Request physical sample if ordering 25+ coins (usually free)
- Check: text clarity, logo detail, size proportions

### Step 4: Production

- 2-3 weeks production time
- Ships via USPS/UPS
- Total timeline: 3-4 weeks from order to delivery
